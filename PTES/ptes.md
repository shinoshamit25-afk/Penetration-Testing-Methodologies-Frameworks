# PTES

Penetration Testing Execution Standard

## Seven Phases

### 1. Pre-Engagement Interactions

- Scope
- Authorization
- Rules of Engagement

### 2. Intelligence Gathering

- Passive Reconnaissance
- Active Reconnaissance

### 3. Threat Modelling

- Identify attack paths
- Prioritize targets

### 4. Vulnerability Analysis

- Discovery
- Validation

### 5. Exploitation

- Initial compromise
- Demonstrate impact

### 6. Post Exploitation

- Privilege Escalation
- Lateral Movement
- Impact Analysis

### 7. Reporting

- Executive Summary
- Technical Findings
- Remediation

## Benefits

Provides a realistic pentesting workflow.
